/ˈseɪ.ri.ə/ (n.)

Cy-Aeria, simply known as Cy is a 22-year-old woman standing at the crossroads between youthful curiosity and the responsibilities of adulthood.

Still learning how to navigate the world, she’s a fresh graduate who's stepped into the ever-changing field of tech as a software developer. When she’s not debugging lines of code, she’s keeping the beat on her drums, sketching lines into her tablet, reading, or immersing herself in digital adventures across games like Genshin Impact, Honkai Star Rail, and Wuthering Waves. Her hands never stop moving, but her heart up until recently, rarely followed.

Cy didn’t mean to find STRXNGER. However, in her constant search for a pulse, she did. Through them, she found how it could feel like being. Like belonging. Like becoming.

On the night she skipped a tech seminar, she wandered into a dim bar with a flickering amp and a rusted drum kit. The mic was broken, the setlist abandoned, but someone was playing anyway. She didn’t introduce herself. She just picked up the sticks and played until her fingers ached and someone across the room started to sing. No one asked who she was, where she was from, or what she did in life. They only cared that she felt it — the tempo, the chaos, the silence between sounds.

In STRXNGER, Cy became The Fool to embody freedom, rebellion, and a new beginning, a fresh start, and the courage to embrace the unknown. She is not a prodigy, and she never claimed to be. She's just a dreamer. One who finally found a place where she could be herself, and where she could create music that resonated with her soul. A place where strangers didn’t stay strangers for long.

Who's STRXNGER?

Some of them were running from things they never talked about. Others were just trying to survive the weight of days that felt the same. They were strangers, not only to each other, but to themselves. Tired hearts, restless hands, quiet minds that once burned bright but had since dulled into silence.
