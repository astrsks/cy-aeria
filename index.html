/ˈseɪ.ri.ə/ (n.)

Cy-areia, known simply as Cy is a 22-year-old woman standing at the crossroads between youthful curiosity and the responsibilities of adulthood.

Having just graduated, she’s stepped into the innovative world of tech as a software developer. But outside her work, her fingers rarely rest—if she’s not tapping away at her keyboard, she’s keeping the beat on her drums, sketching lines into her tablet, reading or immersed in digital adventures across games like Genshin Impact, Honkai Star Rail, and Wuthering Waves.

Using she/her pronouns, Cy’s face claim is MAMAMOO’s Solar. Do note that she is not in any way affiliated with the group or the artist mentioned.
